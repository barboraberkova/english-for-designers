### Storytelling notes English 

This is me. (The head of design at KeySpace)

I grew up as an only child. It wasn’t an intention. I was supposed to have some siblings and play with them. But life had other plans and I am a person who isn’t a sister to someone.

I grew up with no phone. Well, only this pink one with which I could call a magic princess in my imaginary world. NO phones, no iPads. My parents gave me the chance to see the world but also to explore the space to be bored. That gives you a lot of space to be a creative child who is drawing a lot, browsing through a book, building legos, talking to imaginary friends and creating stories.

Kids need other kids to play with. ANd my parents made sure that I had that. I was surrounded by many friends, going on trips, playing in the woods and just being kids. 
Our parents created a giant called Františ and his little dwarf friend Bažináč. They were constantly preparing games for us. One day we went for a treasure hunt, the other day we were just wandering through woods collecting tree pines of leafs. 
We had no phones. We were present for each other. My childhood was the best time of my life and shaped me into the person I am today

Then my parents gave me the chance to study abroad which opened many doors for me. My American dad pushed me and convinced me to study graphic design. My parents supported it and gave me a chance to study at Vškk. 

I started a business. But I guess it wasn’t enough for me. When you’re an ambitious person you’ll always want more. Everything you do is never enough for you.

I started thinking about more business ideas. 

So my ambitions, magical childhood, my American experience and todays world were the cornerstones of ubba.

Nowadays, many kids spend a lot of time on their phones. They have no space to be bored. That doesn’t exist anymore. Today’s world offers a way to spoil kids and give them endless choices that aren’t always the best for them.

To create ubba, I couldn’t copy the past, I had to design for the future and for a different generation.



10 minutes is not a lot of time to introduce something I’ve been working on for the past  YEAR. But I’ll give you at least a short overview and introduce the concept.)

Ubba is a brand for children. I could say it is a toy store. But that isn’t quite right. Ubba is a reaction to today’s fast moving world full of information and things. Ubba wants to give children beautiful and healthy childhood full of stories, experiences, ideas, discovery and many more, because childhood is the most precious thing a person has in life.

Ubba helps parents 

It was a bit challenging at first. I had to define the brand values which can sound a bit abstract to customers. Telling them you have products with good quality, great materials and some benefits is nice. Until you have to tell the customers.

So I came up with the bear family. The bear family represents the brand values and communicates them through their day to day life. They raise the kids by the Montessori and respectful way of parenting. 

Each member stands for something. The kids represent kids and parents parents and the ways of parenting.

I came up with the concept. I created the designs for everything. Designed the website. An app. Everything you can think of. But we will talk about that some other time.

I might not change the world. But I at least want to make an impact in someone else’s life. That is why I created ubba.


So this is my story. My childhood inspired me to create ubba. I want to give other kids the experience I had or at least help the parents to give their kids a little piece of what I had.

My parents are the story behind ubba even if they don’t realize it. They shaped me into who I am today and …

So that’s my story. Thank you for listening to my talk but more importantly: thanks to my parents for raising a child the best way they could.

There is a lot of work to come but I hope that one day, you’ll come to ubba store to say hi with your kids and will buy something for yourself. 

For now, if you’d like to know more about ubba or other projects, reach me. We can work together.
